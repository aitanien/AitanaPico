Hey! I'm Aitana, a cross-disciplinary designer specialised in UI/UX design, based in Valencia and currently working worldwide.

Born in Valencia, Spain and raised in Manchester, UK, I returned to Valencia to study Design in Creative Technologies at La Universidad Politécnica, a degree where I learnt everything from UI/UX, 3D design, basic level programming, marketing, final graphic arts, among other skills.

Since graduating in 2023, I have been working as a UI/UX designer at Sherpas Wealth, a finance platform for wealth management, where I have been able to practice wireframing and prototyping, user journey mapping, benchmarking, and both low and high fidelity visual design to create compelling UX/UI web pages. My background in illustration and graphic communication has allowed me to represent data in interesting ways, and create clean, usable designs.

I'm currently interested in organising chaos, and in experimenting with a wide variety of mediums and aesthetics.
